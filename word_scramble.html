Word Scramble Game

Rules

  • Unscramble the letters to form the correct word.
  • Drag the letters to the dashes to complete the word.
  • You have a limited number of moves to guess the word
  • Unscrambling a word rewards you with coins.
  • In Hard mode, use the clue button to reveal 2 letters.
  • Additional clues can be purchased for 200 coins each.
  • All the best...
